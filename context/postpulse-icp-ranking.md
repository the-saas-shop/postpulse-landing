# PostPulse ICP Ranking: Intent-Based Analysis
**Ranked from Highest to Lowest Purchase Intent**

---

## üìä Intent Scoring Framework

Each ICP is scored on:
- **Pain Intensity** (1-10): How badly they need this solution
- **Budget Availability** (1-10): Ability to pay $19.99/mo
- **Time Urgency** (1-10): How quickly they need to solve this
- **Solution Awareness** (1-10): They know they need content tools
- **Revenue Impact** (1-10): Direct connection to their income
- **Decision Speed** (1-10): How fast they can decide to buy

**Total Intent Score**: Sum of all factors (max 60)

### Focus on Top 3 ICPs (Tier 1)

**Primary Target**: ICP #1 - Monetizing Solopreneurs
- Highest pain, clearest ROI, fastest decision
- Most word-of-mouth potential (they network with each other)
- Willing to pay for proven results

**Secondary Target**: ICP #2 - Time-Strapped Creators with Day Jobs
- Large market size
- Clear pain (time scarcity)
- Future monetizers (will become ICP #1)

**Tertiary Target**: ICP #3 - Early-Stage SaaS Founders
- Strong network effects
- High quality customers (technical, understand tools)
- Will share with other founders
- Long-term value (loyal if it works)

### Tier 2: Test and Optimize

ICPs #4-6 are good targets AFTER you've nailed messaging for Tier 1.
- Lower priority for initial marketing
- Target with specific messaging variations
- May have higher churn, monitor closely

### Tier 3-4: Avoid or Deprioritize

ICPs #7-11 should be deprioritized:
- Higher customer acquisition cost
- Lower lifetime value
- Product-market fit concerns
- Feature gaps for their needs
- Better segments exist (focus there first)
